Fremer Tours and Travel - Site Plan

Site Name

Fremer Tours and Travel was chosen to reflect a professional yet personalized travel company. The name combines a sense of identity ("Fremer") with a clear description of services offered ("Tours and Travel").

Site Purpose

This website will promote Fremer Tours and Travel's services. It will feature curated tour packages, videos and images of destination sites, contact details, and booking options. The goal is to inform and attract potential travelers.

Scenarios

  • What destinations and tour packages does Fremer Tours and Travel offer?
  • How best can I book or contact Fremer Tours and Travel for customized packages?

Color Schema

Dark Blue (#003366): Used for headers, footer background, and navigation.
Orange (#ff6600): Used for call-to-action buttons and highlights.

Typography

Main Font: Segoe UI, used for body and paragraph text for readability.

Headers: Bolded Segoe UI with dark blue color to draw attention.
