Fremer Tours and Travel

Fremer Tours and Travel

This name reflects a tourism business offering adventure, cultural, and wildlife tours across Uganda and East Africa. It was chosen because it represents the brand’s mission to help travelers explore the region’s beauty and diversity.

Optional domain: fremertours.com (availability not required for this class)

Site Purpose

The website will promote Fremer Tours and Travel by showcasing available destinations, customizable tour packages, and travel tips. Visitors will be able to explore itineraries, view photo galleries, read testimonials, and book tours directly through the site’s booking form.

Scenarios

  • What tour packages are available for wildlife safaris in Uganda?
  • How can I book a customized cultural tour experience?
  • Are there travel tips and requirements for visiting Uganda and neighboring countries?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E8B57 (Sea Green) – for headings, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons.

Secondary Color: #F4A460 (Sandy Brown) – for accents, highlights, and background elements.

Neutral Color: #FFFFFF (White) – for text backgrounds and sections requiring clarity.

Typography

Heading Font: Poppins, sans-serif – for titles, navigation links, and section headers.

Body Font: Lato, sans-serif – for paragraphs, captions, and descriptions.
